near.email

Secure, wallet-based encrypted email for the NEAR ecosystem

Wallet-Based Identity

Your NEAR account is your email. alice.near becomes alice@near.email. No signup, no passwords — just your wallet.

End-to-End Encryption

ECIES with secp256k1. Emails encrypted before storage using keys derived via NEAR MPC Chain Signatures. Only the wallet owner can decrypt.

TEE-Protected Server

Server runs inside Intel TDX. Email received → immediately encrypted → original deleted. Operators cannot read your mail.

Gmail Compatible

Full compatibility with Gmail, Outlook, and any email client. Internal NEAR-to-NEAR emails never touch external SMTP servers.
